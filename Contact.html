About Us

Prayaas,IIITA is an earnest attempt to bring sunshine in wearisome lives of not so privileged kids. It is a volunteer movement initiated by student fraternity of IIIT-Allahabad.

Our objective is to spread the light of literacy, making people aware about the importance of literacy.
